# NeuroBubble

### AI-Powered Emergency Self-Healing Communication Network

An intelligent wireless mesh networking platform designed to provide resilient communication during disasters, infrastructure failures, and remote operations through AI-driven routing, predictive network intelligence, and self-healing connectivity.

---

## About The Project

NeuroBubble is designed to solve one of the most critical challenges during emergencies: communication failure.

Natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, cyclones, landslides, and wildfires often damage cellular towers and communication infrastructure, leaving rescue teams and affected communities disconnected when communication is needed most.

Traditional emergency communication systems rely on fixed routing strategies and centralized infrastructure, making them vulnerable to node failures, interference, and network congestion.

NeuroBubble creates a decentralized and intelligent wireless mesh network capable of establishing communication without requiring internet access, cellular networks, or fixed infrastructure.

The platform combines:

* Wi-SUN Mesh Networking
* Edge AI and TinyML
* Self-Healing Communication
* Predictive RF Intelligence
* Adaptive Routing Algorithms

Each node continuously monitors network health and learns from its RF environment to optimize communication paths and maintain connectivity even under challenging conditions.

---

## Why NeuroBubble?

Communication is often the first service disrupted during emergencies.

This can lead to:

* Delayed rescue operations
* Loss of situational awareness
* Inability to send emergency alerts
* Increased response times
* Reduced public safety

Current mesh networking solutions can recover from failures but typically react only after communication has already been disrupted.

NeuroBubble introduces predictive intelligence that enables the network to anticipate failures before they occur and proactively optimize communication routes.

By combining wireless mesh networking with Edge AI, NeuroBubble creates a resilient communication ecosystem capable of adapting to dynamic environments in real time.

## Real-Life Example

Imagine a flood-affected region where cellular towers have stopped functioning.

Rescue teams deploy NeuroBubble nodes across the affected area. Within minutes, the nodes automatically establish a Wi-SUN mesh network and begin exchanging information.

As environmental conditions change, the AI engine continuously monitors signal strength, packet delivery ratio, interference levels, and battery health. If a communication link begins to degrade, NeuroBubble predicts the failure and reroutes traffic before connectivity is lost.

Citizens can transmit SOS messages, rescue teams can coordinate operations, and emergency services can maintain situational awareness despite the absence of conventional communication infrastructure.
